PhotoSweep Support

For support, privacy, billing, or App Review questions, contact support@yukimorikawa.com.

Common Topics

  • Purchases and refunds are handled by Apple. Users can manage subscriptions in Apple account settings.
  • Photos are processed on device and are not uploaded by PhotoSweep.
  • Photos deleted through PhotoSweep may remain in Recently Deleted in Apple Photos before permanent removal.
  • Local app data can be removed by deleting PhotoSweep from the device.
